Nomade - Site Plan

Site Name

Nomade Bike Store was chosen to represent the ciclism spirit on south of Brazil.

Site Purpose

The Chamber of Commerce website serves to:

  • Promote local businesses and provide networking opportunities for sports in our community
  • Encourage our community to improve the sport to their lives, spatially the ciclism.

Scenarios

  • How much cost to start to practice a sport?
  • What are the latest developments in the cycling market?
  • Where i can fix my bike?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used for the website:

  • Primary Color: Very dark (#323031) - Used for headers and navigation.
  • Secondary Color: Light orange (#ffc857) - Used for buttons and accents.
  • Tertiary Color: Very dark cyan (#084c61) - Used for backgrounds and text.
  • Quaternary Color: Dark cyan (#177e89) - Used for links and shaddows for text
  • Quinary Color: Bright red (#db3a34) - Used for text and exclamations on promotions.

Typography

The selected fonts are:

  • Heading Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif.
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif.
